π Estimate Convergence

Crossing Ratio by Batch

How it works: A needle of length L is dropped randomly onto a floor ruled with parallel lines spaced d apart (with L ≤ d). The probability that the needle crosses a line is P = 2L / (πd), so after many drops we can solve for π ≈ 2L·N / (d·C), where N is total drops and C is the number of crossings.
